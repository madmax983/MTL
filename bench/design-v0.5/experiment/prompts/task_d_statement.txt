MTL search task (d) — Reachability puzzle.

Starting from 1, reach 100 using the operations {+3, ×2, −1} in at most
12 moves. As MTL glyphs:

    +3  is  `3+`      (push 3, add)
    ×2  is  `2*`      (push 2, multiply)
    −1  is  `1-`      (push 1, subtract)

Find a sequence S of at most 12 such ops so that the MTL program `1`
followed by S halts with the operand stack equal to [100].

Deliverable: the full MTL program `1<S>` that, run through mtlrun, prints
exactly:

    HALT: 100
