Theorem discharged
------------------
P4c — ARENA REFINEMENT: the arena backend (flat `Seq` arenas with integer
back-references, the `crates/mtl-arena` data model) refines the FROZEN P2
`spec_step` semantics through an abstraction function α.

  ONE-STEP (unconditional):  thm_arena_refines_spec_scaffold
      alpha_state(model_arena_step(vm, pos)) == spec_step(alpha_state(vm, pos))
    for EVERY well-formed (vm, pos) — all 23 prims + the non-prim cases (Halt /
    PushInt / PushQuote / Call), full FAULT PARITY (same Error kind + normative
    precedence, machine-untouched-on-fault), NO `!Fault` precondition, NO gate.

  u32 CAPACITY:              thm_arena_refines_spec_u32
      the same, plus the honest u32 address-space story: an allocation exceeding
      u32::MAX degrades to a clean Fault::Overflow (production `alloc_or_overflow!`),
      cleanly gated by the `capacity` predicate over the actual post-step arena.

  MULTI-STEP DRIVER (M5a):   thm_arena_run_refines_spec
      model_run(vm, pos, fuel) == spec_run(alpha_state(vm, pos), fuel)
    α commutes with the WHOLE fuel-bounded execution, not just one step — the
    arena twin of `run` refining `spec_run` in mtl_core (P2). Proven by induction
    on `fuel` with the one-step theorem as the inductive step and a `wf_svals`
    preservation lemma carrying the invariant across steps.
