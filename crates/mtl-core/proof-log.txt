------------------------------------------------------------

                         (totality of spec_step; no wildcard, no partial match).
  div_semantics_witnesses  Pinned truncating div/mod witnesses (by (compute)):
                         trunc_div(-7,2) = -3, trunc_mod(-7,2) = -1, ...,
                         and i64::MIN / -1 overflows.
  trunc_divmod_correct   General truncating div/mod correctness (nonlinear_arith):
                         a = q*b + r, |r| < |b|, sign(r) = sign(a) or r = 0.
  smoke_dup_apply        P1 determinism witness: the two-token `dup apply` Y-idiom
                         reaches stack [Quote(q)] with continuation q in two steps.

                         Well-formedness + termination (decreases) discharged.
